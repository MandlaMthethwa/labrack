About Us

Sc lab track deals with the maintenance of the student's attendance details. It will generate a weekly report of the student's attendance based on their presence in class. Only if the student is present on that particular period will the system calculate the number of hours the student has attended. This system aims to reduce the paper work and saving time by generating accurate results from the student's attendance, avoid redundant data and mitigate the issue of students signing in for each other. Efficient reports cannot be generated if attendance is maintained manually. Instead as the attendances are maintained in registers it has been a tough task for admin and staff to maintain for long time. the software can keep long and retrieve the information when needed.
